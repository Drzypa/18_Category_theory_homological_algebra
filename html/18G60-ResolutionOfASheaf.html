resolution of a sheaf


Given a sheaf ℱ on a topological space X and a pair (ℱ∙,d∙) where ℱ∙={ℱq}q∈ℕ is a family of sheaves on X and d∙={dq}q∈ℕ is a family of sheaf morphisms

dq:ℱq→ℱq+1,

we say that (ℱ∙,d∙) is a resolution of ℱ if there exists an injection j:ℱ→ℱ0 such that the sequence

0→ℱ⁢→𝑗⁢ℱ0⁢→d0⁢ℱ1⁢→d1⁢⋯→ℱq→…

is exact.
