Tor


Let R be a ring with multiplicative identity. Let M be a () module over R. We may assume there exists an exact sequence P*:

…⁢…→P2→P1→P0

with the Pn projective and the cokernel of the last map M. Given M, this sequence is unique up to chain homotopy. Hence we may make the following definitions.

For a () R- module A we may define

E⁢x⁢tRn⁢(M,A)=Hn⁢(P*;A)

For a (left) R- module A we may define

T⁢o⁢rRn⁢(M,A)=Hn⁢(P*;A)
