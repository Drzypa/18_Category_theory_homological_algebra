proof that an equalizer is a monomorphism


Suppose that f is the equalizer of g and h (refer to the commutative diagram below). Let a,b be morphisms such that

f⁢a=f⁢b=d.

We must show that a=b. Since d equalizes g and h, by definition of equalizer c is the unique morphsim such that d=f⁢c. Hence, both a and b are equal to c, and hence equal to each other.
