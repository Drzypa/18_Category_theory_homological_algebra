section functor


1 Essential data

Let us consider an Abelian category 𝒞 which is locally small and a dense subcategory 𝒜 of 𝒞, with T:𝒞→𝒞/𝒜 being the canonical functor. Moreover, let us assume that T has a right adjoint denoted by S such that one has the following functorial isomorphism, or natural equivalence:

H⁢o⁢m𝒞⁢(X,S⁢(Y))≅H⁢o⁢m𝒞/𝒜

.

Definition 1.1.

The right adjoint functor

S:𝒞/𝒜→𝒞

of T– which is specified by the essential data above– is called a section functor.

Note: the category 𝒜 is defined as a localizing subcategory.
