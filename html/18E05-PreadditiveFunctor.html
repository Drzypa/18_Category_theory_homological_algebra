preadditive functor


Definition 0.1.

A functor between two preadditive categories 𝒜𝒫 and 𝒜𝒫′ is called a (pre) additive (or preadditive) functor, if for any pair of morphisms f,g∈H⁢o⁢m𝒜𝒫⁢(X,Y) one has that F⁢(f+g)=F⁢(f)+F⁢(g).
