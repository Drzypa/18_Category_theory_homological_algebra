C3-category generators corollary


Corollary 0.1.

(Corollary 1.4 in ref. [1])

Let A be an object with a family of generators {Ai} in a C3-category. Then, the finitely generated subobjects of A form a direct family of subobjects in the C3-category whose direct limit lim→⁡{Ai} is A.
