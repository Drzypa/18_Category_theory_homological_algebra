\section{Generalized Hurewicz fundamental theorem}   

The Hurewicz theorem was generalized from connected CW-complexes to arbitrary topological 
spaces \cite{Spanier66} and is stated as follows.

\begin{theorem} ({\em Generalized Hurewicz Fundamental Theorem.})

 \emph{If $\pi_r (K,L) =0$ for $ 1 \leq r \leq  n$ , $(n \geq 2)$, then $h_\pi : \pi_n^* (K,L)\simeq H_n(K,L)$ , where $\pi_n$ are homotopy groups,  $H_n$ are homology groups, K and L 
are arbitrary topological spaces, and `$\simeq$' denotes an isomorphism.}
\end{theorem}
